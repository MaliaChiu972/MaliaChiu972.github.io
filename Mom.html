My Family

This is my Mom. She is one of the Best moms in the world and I don't know what I would do without her in my life. She raised me for most of my life as a single parent.

Long story short I am adopted from China and have lived in America, in the same house since I was 8 months old. I am extremely grateful for all the opportunities that my mom has given me and all the sacrifices she has made for me. I am glad that I got to grow up in an amazing and supportive household, as well as a safe neighborhood/ environment. I know that it is not easy taking care of another living being let alone try and raise and guide them to be the best that they can be.

My mom and I can be opposites sometimes in terms of me being an introvert while she is an extrovert. I do love all the things she got me interested in at a young age. Like animals, sports, and art. When I was young we would play racquetball at health club that she used to work at and teach racquetball there. Sadly both of us a mobility challenged due to personal injuries, but I love whenever we have the time to go for a long walk with the dogs, near the water. I also enjoy just having small talk while watching a show or when helping to prepare food.

When I show my mom this I want her to know how much I love and cherish all the things that she has done for me and know that if I could change anything about how she brought me up I wouldn't change a thing.

I LOVE YOU MOM!!!
